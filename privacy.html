Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This page explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how you can exercise your rights.

Data Controller

Straja.ai - operated by Sorin Manole
Samariterstr. 31, 10247, Berlin, Germany
Email: hello@straja.ai

What we collect

  • Waitlist submissions: your email address and any message you include when using the early-access form.
  • Server logs: standard technical logs (IP address, user agent, referrer) generated by our hosting provider to keep the site secure.

Why we collect it (legal bases)

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): to email you regarding early access and product updates when you join the waitlist.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): to operate, secure, and improve this website (e.g., preventing abuse, understanding aggregate demand).

How we process your data

Waitlist form submissions are transmitted to our inbox via Formspree (a form relay service). Server logs are stored by our hosting provider. We don’t sell your data.

  • Processors:
    • Resend – email delivery service used to process license key requests and send transactional messages (such as confirmations or license key delivery). Data is processed according to their privacy policy:
    • Vercel – serves this website and stores server logs.

Update the provider names above once final.

International transfers

Your data may be processed outside your country. Where applicable, we rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) with our processors.

Retention

  • Waitlist data: retained until you opt out or we no longer need it for early-access communications.
  • Server logs: typically retained for a short period for security and troubleshooting, then deleted or anonymized. Exact periods depend on the hosting provider.

Your rights (GDPR)

You can request access, rectification, deletion, restriction, or portability of your personal data, and you may object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise your rights, email hello@straja.ai. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your country.

Cookies

This site currently uses only essential features to deliver pages and the waitlist form. If we add analytics or non-essential cookies later, we will update this notice and provide controls.

Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to mitigate risks.

Contact

For any privacy questions, contact hello@straja.ai.
